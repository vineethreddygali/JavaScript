| How to bid |

We run online auctions, which typically end in the provided time. Items end, one at a time, in the order they are shown. There are two types of options: there is a bidder, and a person sells items for bidding. Our website is a median platform for both bidders and sellers. In this, we run frequent auctions in large groupings that will all end on a specified date, one item at a time. Bidding is open when the auction goes live, and more things can and will be added up until the closing day. Mark your calendar for the closing day because that's when all the action happens! Strap yourself in and watch the auctions end one at a time, and see if you can score yourself an incredible deal! Our website updated live by the second so that you can see bidding updates from the main auction page, or your watch list, in real-time! You can also place a bid on ANY page where an item is visible! Once the auction ends, you'll receive a combined invoice of all your winnings, with instructions on how to complete your order! The payment/Pickup period. You can bid any time you want once the auction is live and active. MOST part of the action happens on the last day, so it is best to watch the auction live to ensure you don't miss out and to score some fantastic deals on items that sell for a low price. You can quickly bid on an item by one increment by clicking the QUICK BID button. You can keep track of the items you are interested in by adding them to your "Watch List".
